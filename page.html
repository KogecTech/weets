ABOUT WEETS

The Digital Woman Summit is an ANNUAL event geared towards generating new ideas on how best to encourage the women folk (from the girl-child stage to the corporate woman) in strongly keying into and playing outstanding roles towards closing the technology gender gap.

WEETS 2024 is a platform that seeks to highlight opportunities available for women/girls by bridging the digital divide while expanding new ideas on ICT and telecom, thereby positioning them to effectively play a formidable role in the Nigerian tech space. It will also recognize and celebrate the Women on the one hand and Corporate Organizations on the other hand, who are already blazing the trail in Nigeriaâ€™s ICT ecosystem.

THE OBJECTIVES OF WEETS 2024

The primary objectives of the 2024 Women Entrepreneurs & Executives in Tech Summit is to provide a dynamic platform for women across various sectors to explore the intersection of AI and leadership, share insights,experiences, and best practices, and collectively chart a course for advancing gender diversity and inclusion in the tech sector.

SUMMIT OVERVIEW:
The summit will feature a diverse array of keynote presentations, panel discussions,workshops, and networking opportunities, addressing key topics such as:

1. The role of AI in shaping the future of business leadership and innovation.
2. Strategies for fostering diversity, equity, and inclusion in AI development and implementation.
3. Overcoming challenges and barriers to advancement as women leaders in the tech industry.
4. Leveraging AI technologies for business growth, sustainability, and societal impact.
5. Empowering the next generation of women and girls to excel in AI and technology fields.

At WEETS, we believe in the power of live events to bring people together, simulate discussion and debate, and as a forum for networking. The virtual component will help extend the reach and engagement.
